Meet Your Teacher

Passionate About Music Education

Get ready to unleash your inner beat with Rommel, the drum teacher who knows how to make learning fun and exciting. With years of experience as a performer and educator, Rommel brings a wealth of knowledge to the table.

Having graduated with a Bachelor of Music in Percussion, Rommel honed his skills under the guidance of renowned drummer Michael Alba. He has graced the stage as part of the Shoreline Band and collaborated with popular artists like Jireh Lim, rocking music tours and festivals across the Philippines.

Rommel understands that each student is unique, and he caters to their individual needs and learning styles. He believes that everyone has a talent for music, and his goal is to help his students transform their potential into practical drumming skills.

Whether you are aiming for grade exams or simply looking for a stress-relieving hobby, Rommel has you covered. He has a track record of success coaching students through their exams, but he is equally passionate about making drumming a fun and enjoyable experience.
